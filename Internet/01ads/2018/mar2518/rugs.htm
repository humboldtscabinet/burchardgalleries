1007 SEMI-ANTIQUE PERSIAN HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 10'5'' X 13'1'': Good colors and good design are the hallmarks of this lovely old Persian rug. A large lobed central medallion with spandrels is on a brick red field with blue corners and a blue main border.
CONDITION: Wear is consistent with age.
1072 SEMI-ANTIQUE PERSIAN SAROUL HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 9'7'' X 10'9'': This rug has a traditional Sarouk pattern with a design of well-placed clusters of flowers in shades of blue, white, peach, and beige on a salmon colored field.
CONDITION: Wear is consistent with age of rug, has been re-fringed at one end.
1099 APPROX 35-65 YR OLD PERSIAN HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 8'10'' X 11'7'': This vintage rug has a pretty medium blue field with a white and red/rust colored central medallion. The edge of the medallion has sprays of flowers and more sprays are found circling the medallion. The white and red/rust colored corners and the white ground main border complete the design of the rug.
CONDITION: Wear consistent with age but mostly good.
1143 PERSIAN MAHAL HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 9'6'' X 12'7'': Traditional Persian with brick red field, a large central medallion and corners in lighter colors and a black ground main border.
CONDITION: Some color fade and soil but otherwise good.
1156 APPROX 15-35 YR OLD INDO-TURKISH OUSHAK HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 6'3'' X 8'10'': This rug has the traditional design and colors of a pale Oushak rug. The ivory ground of the field has a large-scale design of simple motifs in colors of pale blue, yellow, and beige. The main border has a pale blue ground.
CONDITION: There are some stains and shading.
1165A TURKISH HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 7'4'' X 10'5'': Bold patterned and colored tribal look rug with five large aqua or blue stylized trees on a salmon/rust field. The border has the same colors plus white guard borders.
CONDITION: Mostly good with some slight stains.
1183 APPROX 20-35 YR OLD SINO-FRENCH AUBUSSON HAND WOVEN NEEDLEPOINT RUG, 10'8'' x 15'11'': This large rug has a classical French design with a very pastel palette. The round central medallion has an inner circle of green leaves and an outer circle of leaves and clusters of grapes. The open ivory field is surrounded by octagonal shaped bands or borders. One border has an overall floral motif, and another has long flowering tree branches that alternate with oval panels with vases or other items showcased in the center. The triangular corners that have a crest or shield like design make the octangular rug back into a rectangle. Good condition.
1184 PERSIAN HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 8'4'' x 11'3'': The midnight blue field is covered with a well-drawn, colorful traditional floral Persian design. Pale blue guard and ivory main border complete the rug.
CONDITION: Good with some slight stains.
1226 INDO-PERSIAN HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 8'3'' X 10'7'': This rug has a charming naive design consisting of a variety of stylized flowering trees in different sizes and colors on a blue field with a rust main border.
1239 CHINESE HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 3'' x 6': Fun, funky Chinese rug has two white and green ''Foo'' dogs facing the central design on a cinnamon colored field. Great entryway or conversation piece.
CONDITION: Good.
1253 PERSIAN HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUNNER, 2'5'' x 21'4'': This very long runner has a red ground field that is covered with a repeating light colored floral design framed by ivory and pale blue borders.
CONDITION: Good.
1268 AMERICAN KARASTAN MACHINE MADE WOOL RUG, 7'1'' X 9'11'': This pretty rug has a Persian design complete with a date cartouche at one end. Black, pale yellow, and ivory colors are used in the intricate design on the black field.
CONDITION: good.
1279 INDO-PERSIAN HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 7'8'' X 10': The variegated black field is covered with a design of colorful palmettes and leaves linked by vines. The red main border completes the design.
CONDITION: Some visible wear, soil and fading.
1288 TURKISH OUSHAK HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUNNER, 2'7'' x 8': This soft ivory field runner has an undulating nouveau style repeating design in soft blues and yellow.
CONDITION: Good with some wear of fringe.
1293 CHINESE HAND KNOTTED WOOL RUG, 3'' x 6'1'': Fun, funky Chinese rug with a dragon chasing a flaming pearl at one end and a beautiful Phoenix rising on the other end of the blue/grey field.
CONDITION: Good.
1337 CHINESE HAND KNOTTED SILK RUG, 3' X 5'1'': Five Dragons in shades of blue are on a pale beige field chasing flaming pearls.
CONDITION: Good with some shading.
1367 PERSIAN HAND KNOTTED SILK ON SILK, 8' X 9'10'': The red field has a large central white medallion, white and blue flowers over the field and white and pale blue borders.

1379 TURKISH HAND KNOTTED FAUX SILK (COTTON) RUG, 4'1'' X 6'3'': This rug has a traditional format but uses unusual colors including purple, lavender, blue, white, and gold accents.
