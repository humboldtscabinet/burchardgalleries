INSTRUCTIONS FOR PLACING AN ABSENTEE OR PHONE BID

 

1.       Complete ABSENTEE & PHONE BIDDING APPLICATION and fax or email (24 hrs a day), the signed bid form to Burchard Galleries no later than 5 PM 1 day prior to auction day at (727) 821-1814. 

2.       The bid form must be signed to be considered valid.  In the event that you are not the winning bidder, your credit card number will be destroyed.  A 10% Cash Deposit is also acceptable in lieu of a credit card. 

3.       Call and review your bid form to assure its receipt, legibility and that you have the correct lot numbers listed and a correct contact phone number.

4.       Please assure that you have all necessary descriptions, conditions, etc to make an appropriate decision on your bid.

5.       Your bid number will be assigned by the staff & held in house. 

6.       Phone bids:  You will be called at the number listed (one number only please), one (1) lot prior to the item of your interest.  Please ask for an approximate time of sale.   It is helpful in the event of a phone problem to have a back-up bid listed on the form. If you wish to provide a back-up bid, place a note next to the amount “back-up only”.  Through this action, one gives authorization to the phone bid worker to bid on your behalf up to the maximum limit of their bid following the rules for absentee bidding listed under #7 below. 

7.       Absentee bids: The AUCTIONEER will make every attempt to sell those authorized items at the lowest possible price below the WRITTEN maximum bid by starting the bidding at 75% of the highest absentee bid.

 

TERMS AND CONDITIONS:    PHONE BIDDERS must have a $100.00 minimum starting bid.  Burchard Galleries is apologetic but not liable for missed phone calls and encourages bidders to leave backup absentee bids. 

 

PAYMENT:   Acceptable forms of payment include cash, money orders, bank checks, Visa, MasterCard Discover & American Express cards or wire transfers.  An 18% buyer's premium will be in effect.  Please adjust your bidding to accommodate the Buyer Premium within your auction budget.  The valid credit card number used to guarantee your bid will be charged on Monday following the sale unless you contact us to arrange other payment method.  By signing the attached bid form, you are specifically authorizing Burchard Galleries to charge your account in the amount of your bid, plus 18% Buyer’s Premium together with Florida Sales Tax, if applicable, unless other arrangements are agreed to.  Should you not comply with any other arrangements agreed to, within 4 working days, we will charge your charge card on the 5th day.  Auctioneer reserves the right to negate any sale which does not comply with these terms.

 

MERCHANDISE REMOVAL:   In the event that you are a "winning bidder", we require pick up of merchandise to be prompt.  Next day pick up is not necessary, however, if item(s) are not removed from our facilities by 5pm on the 12th day after the auction,  on the 13th day storage fees in the amount of $10 per day per item will be charged until removal and could result in the items being re-sold for storage fees.  Shipping arrangements (if necessary) must be made as soon as payment is received. All packing and shipping is done by outside shipping companies.  Failure to object to item within 24hrs, upon tracked arrival, constitutes acknowledgment & acceptance of the purchase at the announced price.
