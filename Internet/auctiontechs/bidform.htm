INSTRUCTIONS FOR PLACING AN ABSENTEE OR PHONE BID

To place an ABSENTEE or PHONE BID, you must complete the ABSENTEE AND PHONE BIDDING APPLICATION attached. Use the print command in the file menu of your web browser and Print The Form along with these instructions. A valid application must contain a credit card # (even if you are not going to pay for your purchases with a credit card) and a signature affixed. Your credit card guarantees the goodwill of the bidding. In the event that you are not the winning bidder, your credit card number will be destroyed. A 10% Cash Deposit is also acceptable in lieu of a credit card. All absentee/phone bidders MUST fax a SIGNED copy of the absentee/phone bid application.

Please be very careful when completing the Lot# and Absentee Bid Amount. Many times numbers can be confused and misread, therefore, it is imperative that you review the bid with a BURCHARD staff member to assure accuracy. The complete description of the Item/Lot which is provided within the catalog is also very helpful. You will not receive a bidder card - your number will be assigned by the staff and held in house.

Through this action, one gives authorization to the AUCTIONEER to bid on the assigned individual's behalf up to the maximum limit of their bid. The AUCTIONEER will make every attempt to purchase those authorized items at the lowest possible price below the WRITTEN maximum bid.

PHONE BIDDERS: You will be called while the Auctioneer is selling the item prior to your item of interest. Only completed applications will be processed.
